I approach every project like a detective. I ask a ton of questions. I write everything with pen and paper. I follow every lead. I map out everything out on a giant board. I connect the dots. I find the truth. And I love to reveal my findings to a grand, unsuspecting audience.

I’m a UX researcher. I currently work as a custom research analyst for Mintel’s consulting team. I hold a Certificate of Proficiency in Qualitative Research from the Burke Institute. I started my career in advertising as a brand strategist and market researcher across a variety of B2B and CPG companies, including Coca-Cola, Nestle and Corteva.

User Research

I am energized talking to people that don’t feel represented. In 2022, I received a certification of Proficiency in Qualitative Research from the Burke Institute. I have five years of experience moderating IDIs and focus groups across B2B and CPG clients.

Usability Evaluation

I thrive in the nitty-gritty process of evaluating digital products. I have a professional background in survey development and A/B testing. I love to pressure-test products to understand how they can be improved.

Data Visualization

I am constantly finding new, compelling ways to share data. I’ve turned large data sets into digestible reports. And hundreds of hours of transcripts into a visual journey that captured the empathetic narrative of their consumer.
